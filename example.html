Manga 'Hametsu no Oukoku' Gets TV Anime in 2023

Publisher Mag Garden opened an official website for a television anime adaptation of yoruhashi's Hametsu no Oukoku (The Kingdoms of Ruin) manga on Wednesday.
The website also revealed the main cast, staff, and a key visual (pictured). The anime is scheduled to premiere in 2023.
